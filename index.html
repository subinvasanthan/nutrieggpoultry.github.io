About Us


Sale of Karikozhi (Kadaknath / Black chicken) Eggs – Fresh, Healthy & Home-Bred


NutriEgg Poultry specializes in the sale of karinkozhi eggs, known for their exceptional nutritional value. We raise only black chickens (karinkozhi) to ensure purity and quality, avoiding any cross-breeding with other chicken varieties. Our focus is on providing healthy, nutrient-rich eggs to our customers.


Why Choose Our Eggs?


✅ Rich in Nutrients – Our black chicken eggs are known for their higher protein content, essential vitamins, and minerals, making them an excellent choice for a healthy diet.

✅ Medicinal Benefits – Black chicken eggs are believed to have traditional medicinal properties, supporting immunity, vitality, and overall well-being.

✅ Home-Bred & Naturally Raised – We ensure our chickens are free from harmful chemicals, antibiotics, and artificial feed, allowing them to grow in a stress-free, organic environment.

✅ Fresh & High-Quality – We deliver farm-fresh eggs straight from our poultry to your table, guaranteeing superior taste and freshness.


Whether you're looking for a nutritious daily addition to your diet or special eggs with traditional health benefits, our black chicken eggs are the perfect choice. Try them today and experience the difference in quality and taste!
